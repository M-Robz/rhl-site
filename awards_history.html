† Winners of the Hart, Norris, Vezina, and Calder Trophies are determined strictly by season rank, except in the rare event that a player is obviously ranked inappropriately relative to another (e.g., for the 2009 Vezina, Thomas was ranked 26th with a line of 36/2.10/.933, while Backstrom was ranked 24th with 37/2.33/.923). Comparisons between skaters and goaltenders can only be made according to rank. GP are not taken into account.

‡ In the event of a tie, the player with fewer GP will receive the award. If this fails to resolve the tie, multiple players may win the same award.

* To be eligible for the Calder, a player cannot have played more than 25 games in any single preceding season, nor in six or more games in each of any two preceding seasons.
